Running Plants

Arkansas Nuclear One 1 & 2

The operator of this nuclear power plant is Entergy with a power capacity of production of 836 MW for the unit 1 and 988 MW for the unit 2.

Both reactors are PWR but unit 1 is a model Babcock & Wilcox (DRY-Cont) and the unit 2 is a Combustion Engineering (DRY).
